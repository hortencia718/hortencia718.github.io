Glacier Experience is a small group experience that offers an easy glacier hike on Solheimajokull glacier with an experienced glacier guide and is available all year around. The tour offers a pick-up from Reykjavik or a meet on location at
Solheimajokull glacier. This popular day tour is the perfect way to get in real contact with the true reason behind the name Ice-land!
